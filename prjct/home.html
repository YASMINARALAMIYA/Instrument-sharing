About

This application will depend on the area therefore farmers will not be able to communicate with remote farmers through this application. Therefore, this application will only be helpful for nearby farmers to find their cost effective nearby distance. Farmers who will lend agricultural equipment through this application should be careful not to demand more money. Those who lend will benefit however it will certainly be a limited part and it will not be extra in any way. If the lender demands more money, then the borrower will not be willing to borrow.

The borrower must be able to borrow at a lower cost and the options in this application. This application has been designed keeping in mind that the lender can make a profit and the borrower can take a loan at a lower cost. We will solve this through the knapsack algorithm. This algorithm will be able to solve this problem in the interest of both borrower and lender from calculating distance and cost .
